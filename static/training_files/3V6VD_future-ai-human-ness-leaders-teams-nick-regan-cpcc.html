Q. WHAT’S (ON THE) UP in the HR & L&D communities?

Earlier this week I participated in an immersive 2-day retreat in the beautiful city of Barcelona. We were there with an amazing team of 6 faculty members and 32 outstanding alumni from the Nyenrode Business University and IE Business School Global L&D and HR Leadership programs!

Here are my 2 key take aways and an inquiry from these wonderful days...

Key Takeaways:

1.     AI is (OF COURSE) on the UP!!!

I know, I know, I know,,,, this isn’t news… or at least I hope it isn’t news for you. But during the Alumni program I was pretty blown away by the many exciting user cases across the HR & L&D communities. 

Everything from automating ‘skills based’ processes across global companies, to practicing tough feedback conversations with your AI Personal avatar, creating detailed presentations in seconds with knowledge input from all regions / organization functions and developing onboarding training videos within minutes….

AI is here and it is on the UP, changing our world and our ways of working every single day!! 

2.     Re-skilling / Upskilling for Human-ness is on the UP!!!

As we (humans) embrace and collaborate with AI, there is a realization that the skills we need are very much focused on Human-ness.

Human-ness for me is being creative, being curious, being agile, being flexible, being resilient, being self-aware, being open to change, being together, learning and sharing.

We experienced this Human-ness during the many connections that made up the alumni program,,,, in plenary discussions, in breakout discussions, in power workshops, in peer to peer coaching sessions, during network lunches, wonderful / authentic conversations at evening dinners, on adventures as we searched the streets for late night bars (Go Team Cava!), dancing amongst the nightclubbing Barcelona youngsters on the nightclub dance floors (until far too late) and jogging with my running buddies along the beach (far too early.) 

Human-ness is here, it is ever more important and we need to keep it and upskill it as we embrace the world with AI.

My inquiry:

During the program we often discussed Leadership and Leaders and Leadership Development and Leadership mindset…. etc etc etc… I know that 'Leadership' is important …. It is.. I agree.

AND it’s also so very individual. A leader is an individual. What does a Leader really deliver? On his / her own.

Alone, he or she won’t deliver that much…. Right..?

It's the Leader and their team that delivers! This is most certainly right!!

My favorite quote of the program came from the brilliant program Leader, Prof Dr Nick van Dam… he was asked “What inspires you to keep building the program and this alumni.’’

His answer “Well obviously I’m really so passionate about this subject, but maybe more than that… I just love working with this team, my friends.”

Here is my inquiry ….

Why don’t we focus as much on developing teams as we do on developing leaders…?

Really interested to hear your thoughts on this my friends...
