The Joy of Receiving an Unexpected Gift From Your Team Mates

Created on 2020-07-29 15:01

Published on 2020-07-29 15:09

A couple of weeks ago I received a 10 year visa to stay working here in the Netherlands. PHEW,,, this was quite a relief after some months of uncertainty due to Brexit.

 I was happy to share this good news with family, friends and some of my business clients. I also shared the good news with my Friday evening football buddies…. YES!! Really great to sign up for another 10 years of football together!

 Last Friday I went along to the game and before starting the guys called me and the whole team over to the side of the pitch. They made a team circle, asked me to stand in the middle and said they had a small gift for me as they were really happy that I could stay in NL.  They presented me an official 1978 Dutch football shirt! It was completely unexpected and I was completely bowled over / emotional.  I mean... an official 1978 Dutch football shirt! … Does a better gift (or football team) even exist..?

For the last few days and even now writing this, I can still feel the joy. It’s kind of ridiculous ‘for a football shirt’, but I really love football since I was a small child…. & I’m really no expert on how long or deep joy can last in the body,,,,, but this feels like it will stay a whole lot of longer than these few days.

During the last months I’ve been learning (a lot) about ‘teams’ and ‘team coaching’ in a business environment. I learnt about team effectiveness, team dynamics, team motivation, team leadership, team design, team launches, and systems of teams, etc etc etc,,, there is a lots to learn about teams.

I also learnt that the best team coaches are truly authentic and bring their whole self and all their past experiences to support their teams. 

Therefore I’m sharing the joy of receiving this ‘unexpected gift’ from my football buddies with my Linkedin Network … in the future I will certainly encourage the teams that I work with and coach to share ‘unexpected gifts’ with each other. 

Not sure everybody wants a 1978 Dutch football shirt,,, but choosing the right gift for your team mate and presenting it when they are completely not expecting it, can create a truly deep relationship bond in your team.
