I love using metaphors with the teams and individuals when I am coaching. The images that appear help take people out of their heads and into their emotions.... it's beautiful to see & experience.

This morning I was having a run and thought let's have some fun on Linkedin and share / collect some favorite metaphors from the Linkedin community. Of course I could have googled a list,,, but this is more fun.

From the above image on my run this morning, you can see one of my favorites.... BRIDGE(S). And YES, for context, I was educated as an engineer , spent many years engineering and work with many engineers :-))

I would introduce the metaphor like this to the team or client.... 'If we look at this situation using a bridge or bridges as a metaphor.... how do you see it?''

So yes, bridges, they can take us places.... right? Sometimes they are long, sometimes there are a series of bridges and sometimes they are too far away,,,, which is also good to realise... right?

Anyway, enough about bridges.... Really interested to hear about your 'go to' metaphors.... add them in the comments below :-)
